2023 Indian Premier League

The 2023 Indian Premier League (also known as Tata IPL 2023 for sponsorship reasons and sometimes referred to as IPL 2023 or IPL 16) was the 16th season of the Indian Premier League, a franchise Twenty20 cricket league in India. It is organised by the Board of Control for Cricket in India.[1] In the final, Chennai Super Kings defeated Gujarat Titans, by five wickets (DLS method) to win their fifth league title.[2]

Background

On 17 February 2023, the BCCI announced the schedule for the 2023 season of the Indian Premier League.[6] 12 venues were scheduled to host the group stage. The schedule for the playoffs was announced on 21 April. M. A. Chidambaram Stadium in Chennai was chosen to host qualifier 1 and the eliminator whereas the Narendra Modi Stadium was chosen to host qualifier 2 and the final.[7][8] Chennai Super Kings and Gujarat Titans played the final on 29 May 2023.[9] The Super Kings won the match by five wickets (DLS method).

groupstage

Gujarat Titans started their season with a 5 wicket win over Chennai Super Kings at the Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad.[12] After a victory over Delhi Capitals they were defeated by Kolkata Knight Riders.[13] Then Titans defeated the Punjab Kings before they were defeated by the Rajasthan Royals.[14] They went on a three match winning streak after that loss but lost three of their last five matches. They ended the group stage with 10 wins and 4 losses, finishing first on the points table for the second consecutive season.[15] Chennai Super Kings started their season with a loss of 5 wickets against Gujarat Titans at the Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad.[16] After a winning streak of 2 matches they were defeated by Rajasthan Royals in their fourth fixture.[17] Then after a three match winning streak they were again defeated by Rajasthan Royals.[18] They won three of their last five matches. They ended the group stage with 8 wins and 5 losses excluding a washed out match against the Lucknow Super Giants[19] thus, securing second position on the points table.[20]
